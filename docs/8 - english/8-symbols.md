## Quotes

- `\``— backtick (grave accent)
- `' '` — apostrophe / single quote
- `" "` — double quote

---

## Math signs

- `~` — tilde
- `—` hyphen / minus
- `=` — equals sign
- `+` plus sign
- `%` — percent sign
- `< >` — less‑than / greater‑than

---

## Brackets

- `(` — left parenthesis
- `)` — right parenthesis
- `{` — left curly brace
- `}` — right curly brace
- `[` — left square bracket
- `]` — right square bracket

---

## Punctuation

- `!` — exclamation mark
- `:` — colon
- `;` — semicolon
- `.` — period / dot
- `?` — question mark
- `,` — comma
- `—` asterisk

---

## Specific

- `@` — at sign
- `#` — hash / number sign / pound
- `$` — dollar sign
- `^` — caret
- `&` — ampersand
- `_` — underscore
- `/` — slash / forward slash
- `\` — backslash
- `|` — vertical bar / pipe
